# MakeItWork Global

Building infrastructure for the emerging AI and decentralized economy.

MakeItWork Global develops technology that enables humans and autonomous AI systems to safely collaborate, transact, and operate across digital environments.

## Technology Ecosystem

AgentGuard.me  
Runtime monitoring and governance infrastructure for AI agents.

QuantumCache  
Decentralized verification and settlement infrastructure for digital and financial events.

QCASH.ME  
Programmable wallet infrastructure for humans and AI agents.

## Mission

Build practical tools that allow humans and intelligent systems to work together safely and productively.
